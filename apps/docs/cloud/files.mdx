Any file that is used in your emails like assets, images, etc. can be managed on JSX Mail Cloud.

The [JSX Mail framework](/framework/learning/getting-started) will basically upload everything to your account.

You can manage your files on the [JSX Mail Cloud](https://cloud.jsxmail.org/app/files)
